About Us

TheGardenShop.ie, located in Portarlington, Co. Laois, was founded in 2011 to provide a wide range of affordable garden products across Ireland. We focus on offering great value and excellent customer service, which has helped us grow quickly in the Irish market. Whether you're a beginner starting with small pots or an experienced gardener, we have the tools and supplies for you. We aim to make gardening accessible to everyone by offering high-quality products at discounted prices. With over 2500 products in stock, we continue to search for top-quality tools, unique plants, and handy equipment at low prices. We carry well-known brands like Felco, Spears & Jackson, and Wolf Garden. In 2016, we upgraded our website to improve your online shopping experience. In 2021, we opened the SOLAS eco garden shop in Portarlington, offering eco-friendly products along with all your favorite garden items. If you need help or advice, feel free to contact us. We're always ready to assist you, and our experts are happy to share gardening tips and advice. You can also explore our growing library of gardening articles and videos.
